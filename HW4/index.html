The Wolf, the Goat, and the Cabbage

You are a traveler who needs to cross a river, along with your wolf, your goat, and your supply of cabbage. Your boat is only big enough to hold yourself and one of these three tag alongs. The wolf wants to eat the goat, and the goat wants to eat up all the cabbage, but both animals will behave as long as you are right there on the same side with them. How can you cross the river with everything intact?
